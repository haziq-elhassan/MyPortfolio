Malaysia Car Registration 2025 (January - March)

This report analyzes the vehicle registration trends in Malaysia for the first quarter of 2025. A total of 198,378 vehicles were registered between January and March. The trend shows a steady month-on-month increase, with registrations rising from approximately 53,000 in January, to around 67,000 in February, and nearly 77,000 in March. This upward movement indicates positive growth in the automotive sector, potentially driven by market confidence, economic stability, or seasonal purchasing behavior.

Global Cybersecurity Threats (2015-2024)

The Global Cybersecurity Threats Dashboard (2015–2024) highlights significant trends in global cyber incidents. Over the past decade, 3,000 cyberattacks have been reported, affecting more than 1.5 billion users and causing financial losses exceeding $151 billion. Attack frequency peaked between 2017–2018 and again during 2020–2022, reflecting the impact of increased remote work.
